We are two thirds of the way through! Now you know 79 Toki Pona words, plus some names.

Toki Pona isn't just a language people write stories in. Many people speak it online, chatting with one another. To wrap up Part 2 of this course, let's try to read a casual conversation:

There is still much to learn, but now you can hold a conversation, ask and answer questions, and ask your partner to help you out. Toki Pona is most pleasant when you have got someone to enjoy it together with. So go out into the community, meet new people, make friends!

If you want to combine listening and talking, join a [VRChat](https://bsky.app/profile/tokiponavr.bsky.social) meetup, where people can hang out as virtual avatars! Every Saturday some Toki Pona speakers and learners come to enjoy our time together, and talk about things we care about in this nice language.
