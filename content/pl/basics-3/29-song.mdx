You've learned 131 words, and all the important grammar! So what now?

**Join a Toki Pona community!** [ma pona](https://discord.gg/6zkugJFU4x) and [kama sona](https://discord.gg/ChC6qtVsSE) are great places to find people to talk to, and to practice. You can even [hang out in VRChat](https://vrc.group/TOKI.9663)!

**Stay informed!** Every two weeks we post a [newsletter](https://sin-kulupu.pona.la/) about new things happening. Sign up so you don't miss cool events, like the [24 hour celebration stream](https://suno.pona.la/) we have every August!

**Listen to spoken Toki Pona!** [kalama sin](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLjOmpMyMxd8Qs2mAXcLk817tQy_AQj09u) is a podcast where you can listen to live conversations between proficient Toki Pona speakers. Turn on subtitles if it's difficult to keep up!

**Read more Toki Pona!** [lipu tenpo](https://liputenpo.org/) publishes a new zine every month or two, with short stories and talks.

And finally, **listen to [Toki Pona music](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLeCE5N29ioyUbj_lvYm9IdGJnE2HPacVv)!** We have lots of talented musicians writing original songs or translating songs they love. Let's check out this awesome song *'ale li ante'* by jan Nikoleta (kqa)!
